# Parent and Child Robotic Arts and Crafts

Join us for an exciting hands-on workshop where parents and children team up to explore the intersection of robotics and creative arts. This family-friendly event is designed to spark curiosity, foster collaboration, and introduce fundamental robotics concepts through fun, accessible craft activities.

## Event Overview

Parents and children will work together to build simple robotic art projects, learning basic engineering principles while expressing creativity. All materials are provided—no prior robotics experience needed!

**Date:** Upcoming TBD  
**Time:** 9:00 AM – 12:00 PM  
**Location:** HDSI Lab 3.0, UC San Diego  

## Activities

Participants will engage in guided activities including:

- Building simple circuit-based art projects
- Creating moving sculptures with motors
- Decorating and personalizing robotic creations
- Learning about basic electronics in a safe, fun environment

## What to Bring

- Comfortable clothes that can get a little messy
- A creative spirit and willingness to explore
- All materials and tools will be provided
