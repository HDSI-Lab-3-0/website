# Pre-Programmed Robotic Arts & Crafts

## Summary

Robotics + programming concepts taught without requiring computers or internet access.

## K-12 Learning Goals

Computational thinking + robotics fundamentals in low-resource settings.

## Undergraduates Learning

Inclusive design + accessible curriculum constraints.

## Overview

Participants will gain exposure to programming concepts without a computer. This is intended for middle schools with unreliable internet or computer access. The pre-programmed version allows students to still gain exposure to robotics and programming fundamentals.

## Activity Details

- **Focus**: Programming concepts without computers
- **Accessibility**: No computers or internet required
- **Target**: Schools with limited technology access
- **Setting**: Classroom or outreach sites
- **Duration**: 60 minutes
